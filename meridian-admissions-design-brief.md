# Meridian Admissions Website - Comprehensive Design Brief

## Project Overview
Create a modern, sophisticated website for Dr. Abha Sinha's elite college counseling service, Meridian Admissions. The site must position this as a premium, boutique service for ambitious students seeking admission to top-tier universities, graduate schools, and competitive scholarships. The key differentiation is personal, one-on-one attention from Dr. Sinha herself—no teams, no handoffs, no templates.

---

## Brand Positioning & Key Messages

### Primary Value Proposition
"Personal Attention. Strategic Insight. Proven Results."

- College admissions are more competitive than ever
- Dr. Sinha helps students craft their unique story to stand out from the crowd
- Gain admissions to the top colleges, graduate schools, and scholarships in the country
- Unlike larger services with multiple people working on applications, Dr. Sinha personally oversees everything and walks students through every step
- Her approach is crafting a unique, authentic story around each student
- No teams, no handoffs, no templates—she helps craft applications that showcase authentic voice and strategic strengths

### Target Audience
Both students and parents seeking admission to elite institutions

## Design Specifications

### Color Scheme

**Sophisticated Blues:**
- **Primary:** Deep navy blue (#1e3a5f or similar)
- **Secondary:** Medium blue (#4a7ba7 or similar)
- **Accent:** Gold (#d4af37) for CTAs and highlights
- **Neutral:** Warm white/cream backgrounds (#f8f9fa)
- **Text:** Charcoal gray (#333333)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Serif font (like Playfair Display, Crimson Text, or Lora) for sophistication
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (like Inter, Source Sans Pro, or Open Sans) for readability
- **Hierarchy:** Clear distinction between H1, H2, H3, and body text

### Visual Style
- **Modern but timeless:** Avoid trendy elements that will feel dated
- **Elegant and prestigious:** Should feel like an elite service
- **Clean and uncluttered:** Plenty of white space
- **Professional but warm:** Approachable yet authoritative
- **High-end boutique feel:** Not corporate, not generic

### UI Elements
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners (subtle, not overly round), clear hover states
- **Cards:** Subtle shadows, clean borders
- **Icons:** Simple, elegant line icons (not too playful)
- **Logo Display:** University and scholarship logos should be clean, organized, professional (not cluttered)

### Responsive Design
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Mobile navigation should be clean and accessible
- Maintain hierarchy and readability on all screen sizes

### CTAs Placement (Strategic, Not Overwhelming)
1. Hero section - "Book Your Free Consultation"
2. After About section (optional) - "Learn More About Services"
3. Below packages - "Book Your Free Consultation"
4. Final CTA section before contact form - "Book Your Free Consultation"

## Key Implementation Notes

### Content Priority
1. **Immediate credibility:** University and scholarship logos must be prominent
2. **Personal touch:** Emphasize Dr. Sinha's direct involvement throughout
3. **Proven results:** Track record should be unmistakable
4. **Clear differentiation:** One person, no teams, authentic storytelling

### User Experience
- **Easy navigation:** Users should be able to quickly find pricing and contact info
- **Trust building:** Every section reinforces credibility and personal attention
- **Clear CTAs:** Make it easy to book a consultation at key decision points
- **Scannable content:** Use hierarchy, spacing, and visual breaks effectively

### Technical Considerations
- Fast loading times (optimize images, especially logo bars)
- Accessible design (WCAG compliance)
- SEO optimized (meta descriptions, alt tags, structured data)
- Contact form should have proper validation and confirmation
- Analytics tracking for conversions
